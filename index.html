The Product

Flat Light
Redefined.

Our Flat Lights are ultra-lightweight and slim, built with a unique foldable aluminium construction for effortless transport. Mount them on a tripod, hang them from ropes, or crane them 30 metres in the air — they perform flawlessly anywhere.

Panels can be assembled into any custom dimension — both width and height are modular in 4-foot increments, scaling from a compact 4×4 ft unit to massive multi-panel arrays covering entire studios or outdoor scenes.

Why Flat Light

Built for
Professionals.

2.5 kg per Panel

Each 4×2 ft panel weighs just 2.5 kg. The foldable aluminium extrusion frame lets a single person carry, rig, and fly a setup that rivals fixtures ten times heavier.

DMX / RDM Ready

Full 8–16bit DMX512 control with RDM support for remote addressing and diagnostics. Plug straight into any professional lighting console — no extra interfaces needed.

Modular — Any Size

Panels tile in 4-foot multiples with no gaps. Build a compact 4×2 ft key light or a cinema-scale 24×16 ft soft-light ceiling — the same units, infinitely scalable.

Outdoor Ready

Lightweight enough to crane 30 m in the air, tough enough for snow, rain and mud. 250 W per panel scales to any production wattage without compromise.

Every Colour.
